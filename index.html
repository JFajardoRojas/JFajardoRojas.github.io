Computational Materials & Machine Learning

I’m a scientist working at the intersection of machine learning and materials design. My current research focuses on the synthesizability of metal–organic frameworks (MOFs), bridging AI predictions with real-world synthesis and formulation of soft and hard materials.

I hold a PhD in Chemical Engineering (experimental design of fluid–fluid interfaces) and I’m completing a second PhD in Computational Materials Science.
