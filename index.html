About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Colorado School of Mines in Eric Toberer's group. I received a PhD in Materials Science from Colorado School of Mines in 2025 under the guidance of Diego Gómez-Gualdrón. Previously, I received a PhD in Engineering from Universidad de los Andes in Colombia advised by Oscar Alvarez and Diego Pradilla. I specialize in computational materials science, chemical engineering, and machine learning. My work focuses on the synthesizability of materials and bridging AI predictions with experimental realization.
