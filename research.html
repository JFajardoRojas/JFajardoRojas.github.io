Research

I work on computational materials science with a focus on porous materials and soft matter. My group leverages machine learning and statistical mechanics to connect structure, properties, and synthesize-ability.

Current Directions

  • MOF Synthesizability: Learning which linker–metal combinations are practically realizable.
  • Inverse Design: Diffusion and VAE models to propose candidate frameworks under constraints.
  • Interfacial Phenomena: Data-driven models for fluid–fluid interfaces and wetting.

Open Science

We embrace reproducibility. Code and data are shared whenever possible.
